## Overview

At Cylinder Health, I lead the Member Experience squad and oversee all front-end engineering flows across the organization. Cylinder is a Series B digital health company focused on digestive health, serving over 100,000 members.

## My Role

As Engineering Manager, I own the full member journey: from the moment someone registers through their onboarding experience to their first clinical appointment. Every client-facing feature passes through my technical review.

I also manage a 5-person engineering team that I built from scratch. I hired every member, established the engineering culture, and created processes that allow the team to ship with autonomy and confidence.

## Key Initiatives

**Member Onboarding Optimization**: Responsible for the end-to-end registration and onboarding flow, reducing friction and improving conversion through data-driven iteration.

**AI/LLM Integration**: Architected and delivered HIPAA-compliant LLM-powered features integrated into the existing platform, using an event-driven architecture with Firebase, Pub/Sub, and Go microservices.

**Company Rebrand**: Led a high-stakes frontend rebuild coordinating cross-functionally with contractors, Product, Design, and executive stakeholders to deliver on schedule.

**Mobile CI/CD Migration**: Spearheaded the migration to Expo Application Services (EAS), a major project spanning both codebase refactoring and infrastructure changes that improved build reliability and deployment velocity.

## Impact

Built and scaled the engineering team from scratch, doubling team size while maintaining a culture of autonomy, continuous learning, and technical excellence. Launched an internship program and community-of-practice initiatives that measurably improved team retention and skill growth.
